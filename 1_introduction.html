Laws Etc.

List of types in RDA 6.29.1.1

  1. Laws, etc.
  2. Adminsitrative regulations, etc. that are not laws
  3. Court rules
  4. Constitutions, charters, etc. of intergovernmental and non-jurisdiictional bodies
  5. treaties
  6. law reports, citations, digets, etc.
  7. court proceedings, etc.

Not Laws Etc.

  • legislative enactments and decrees of political jurisdictions (including fundamental laws such as constitutions, charters, etc.)
  • decrees of a chief executive having the force of law (all hereinafter referred to as laws).
  • Adminsitrative regulations that are not laws
  • Court rules
  • Treaties
