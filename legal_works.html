Creators

Scope - RDA 19.2.1.1

A creator is a person, family, or corporate body responsible for the creation of a work.

Corporate Bodies Considered to Be Creators

Categories of Creators: 19.2.1.1.1

One or more of the following categories:

  1. Works of an administrative nature
  2. Works that record the collective thought of the body
  3. Works that record hearings conducted by legislative, juducial, governmental and other corporate bodies.
  4. Works that report the collective activity of (i) a conference, or (ii) an expedition, or (iii) an event.
  5. Works from the collective activity of a performing group
  6. Cartographic works from a corporate body

Legal Categories

Categories of Creators: 19.2.1.1.1

  1. Legal works of the following types
    1. laws
    2. decrees of a head of state, chief executive, or ruling executive body
    3. bills and drafts of legislation
    4. administrative regulations, etc.
    5. constitutions, charters, etc.
    6. court rules
    7. charges to juries, indictments, court proceedings, and court decisions
